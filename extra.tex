\noindent\textbf{Example: Column rank of a matrix}
Let
\[
A = \begin{pmatrix} 4 & 7 & 1 & 8 \\ 3 & 5 & 2 & 9 \end{pmatrix}.
\]

The column rank of $A$ is the dimension of the span of its columns:
\[
\text{span}\left(
\begin{pmatrix} 4 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix},
\begin{pmatrix} 7 \\ 5 \end{pmatrix},
\begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{pmatrix},
\begin{pmatrix} 8 \\ 9 \end{pmatrix}
\right).
\]
